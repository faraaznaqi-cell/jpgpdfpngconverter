Why Resize Images?

Proper image resizing is crucial for web performance, faster loading times, and optimal display across different devices and platforms.

Optimal Dimensions for Different Uses

  • Website Headers: 1200-2000 pixels wide
  • Blog Images: 800-1200 pixels wide
  • Social Media: Platform-specific dimensions (Instagram: 1080x1080, Facebook: 1200x630)
  • Email Attachments: 800-1000 pixels wide
  • Thumbnails: 150-300 pixels wide

Maintaining Aspect Ratio

Our tool automatically maintains aspect ratio to prevent distortion. When you adjust width, height adjusts proportionally.

Quality Preservation Tips

Always resize from the highest quality original available. Avoid enlarging images more than 150% as it can cause pixelation.
